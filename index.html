Mastery in Borehole Drilling

Water City Ltd utilizes state-of-the-art rotary drilling rigs, hydrogeological surveys, and borehole logging equipment to locate and extract groundwater with exceptional precision. Our team of certified hydrogeologists has executed over 450 successful boreholes across varied terrains — from rocky highlands to sedimentary basins.

We offer complete turnkey solutions: site assessment, drilling, casing installation, gravel packing, development, pumping tests, and water quality analysis. Each borehole is designed to provide sustainable yield of 2,000 to 20,000 litres per hour, with depths reaching up to 250 meters. We adhere to Uganda National Water & Sewerage Corporation standards, ensuring long-term reliability for communities, farms, and industries.

Additionally, we integrate solar pumping systems directly into boreholes, reducing operational costs by 80%. Our aftercare includes remote monitoring, periodic maintenance, and rehabilitation services. With Water City, you get a water asset that lasts decades.
